Volunteer

At Deevy Care, we rely on dedicated volunteers to help us fulfill our mission of providing compassionate care to elderly homeless individuals. Whether you're interested in providing direct care, organizing events, or fundraising, there are plenty of ways to get involved and make a difference.

How You Can Help:
  • Direct Care: Assist with daily activities, provide companionship, and offer emotional support to our residents.
  • Event Organization: Help plan and coordinate social activities, workshops, and special events for our residents.
  • Fundraising: Organize fundraising campaigns, events, and initiatives to support our programs and services.
  • Administrative Support: Assist with office tasks, data entry, and communication to ensure smooth operations.
  • Community Outreach: Raise awareness about our organization, recruit volunteers, and engage with the community.

No matter your skills, interests, or availability, there's a place for you in our volunteer team. Join us in making a positive impact in the lives of elderly homeless individuals.
